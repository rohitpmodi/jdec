What We Do

At Jeevandeep, all our products are a combination of print and technology to create a holistic educational experience based on global education standards. To cater to the ever evolving needs of our clients, we keep updating our product range. We have an extensive variety of books, digital content and notebooks for schools which have been created based on our editorial expertise, extensive experience and specific requirements of our clients. Through our diverse products, we play a significant role in students’ lives – right from their childhood till they are young adults, ready to embark on a brand new journey.

  • PUBLICATIONS

    Publications

    Jeevandeep books are a hallmark of the greatest quality in education. A great testimony to this is the fact that they are used in over 15000 schools across India and abroad. From pre-school to high school (K-12), we create various books in numerous languages including work books, textbooks, drawing books, books on IT and computer, general knowledge books, atlas, map books, lab manuals and so on. For our brand Periwinkle, we create innovative print titles for children based on international standards of learning. We have books on various subjects including English, Mathematics, Environmental Studies, Nursery Rhymes, etc. Periwinkle’s main aim is to lay a robust ground in children’s early education so they can have a brighter future.

  • E-LEARNING

    E-Learning

    Being digitally empowered is the new way of life, and at Jeevandeep, we wholeheartedly embrace this notion.

    We strongly believe that e-learning makes education an interactive, engaging and fun activity, thereby helping in higher and long-term knowledge retention. Thanks to its accessibility 24X7, it also gives a chance to students to learn at their own pace and rhythm. Of course, as the technology develops further, e-learning is set to become even more interactive and interesting, and full of exciting possibilities.

  • E-LEARNING ONLINE

    E-Learning Online

    Our online e-learning includes our e-learning website for kids www.e-periwinkle.com which has animated videos on nursery rhymes, current affairs, and subjects including English, Maths, Social Studies, Science, EVS, etc. With voice and illustrations, the videos infuse a spark of life into the words of textbooks. An enriching and self-paced learning experience, these videos handpick examples from everyday life along with great quality content, to be a comprehensive learning tool for students all year round. We also have a dedicated Youtube channel www.youtube.com/PeriwinkleKids which has a host of features including nursery rhymes, kindergarten stories, grammar lessons, alphabets modules, Math concepts and much more.
