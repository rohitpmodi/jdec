Our Story

Jeevandeep Prakashan is one of the leading and most trusted educational publishers based in India, with a global imprint through markets like UAE, South Africa and Sri Lanka. While we are headquartered in the maximum city of Mumbai, our offices, totaling to a number of 22 branches, are spread across the length and breadth of the country. Our state-of-the-art manufacturing facility is located in Navi Mumbai.

Through years of hard work, perseverance and constantly re-inventing ourselves, we currently have more than 1500 running titles in books, spread across all boards (CBSE, ICSE and SSC) for grades K-12.

Each and every product of Jeevandeep is created after extensive research by a highly qualified and experienced team of professionals which includes over 200 talented and dignified authors. Our editorial team applies global standards of education to create content that’s par excellence.

Thanks to our fine marketing and sales team that ensures that Jeevandeep not only reaches great heights across the country, but also becomes one of the best publishing brands globally, we have a strong distribution reach that boasts of about 15000 schools and 1500 booksellers.

In the wake of globalisation, Jeevandeep introduced its brand Periwinkle in the year 2008 with an aim to expand its horizons to newer international educational methods, practices and approaches. With Periwinkle, we aspire to introduce the students to these international standards of learning that will help lay their foundation and expose them to the global trends of education.

At Jeevandeep, we put a strong emphasis on the power of digital education and most of our print content is also available in various digital formats like e-books, online videos, CD Roms, pen drives, among others.

Our Family 45 years of spreading knowledge

Our journey began in 1971 with two brothers, Shantilal Furia and Hasmukh Furia, whose experience in retail and wholesale of educational books and deep personal interest in the field of education led to the inception of an independent publishing house called Jeevandeep Prakashan. Soon after, their younger brother Harish Furia, with an educational background in printing technology and an experience of running a printing press, joined them to take the business forward.

The 90s heralded the beginning of a new, younger wave in the business when Parin Furia joined the company in early 1990. After completing his MBA education, Jeet Furia became the youngest to join the family business in 2008.
